Snow Lake, Snoqualmie Region

Location: Snoqualmie Pass

Length: 7.2 miles, roundtrip

Elevation: Gain: 1800ft.
Highest Point: 4400ft.

Snow Lake is a translucent lake belonging to the Alpine Lakes Wilderness area. This is a very popular hike just 52 miles east of downtown Seattle, near Snoqualmie Pass.

Aside from the beauty of the lake, you will also be able to see majestic views of Roosevelt Peak, Chair Peak, and many waterfalls!

Beware of hiking on the Snow Lake trail during the winter. Ensure that you have prior experience in avalanche terrain or are traveling with someone who does when there is snow present.
